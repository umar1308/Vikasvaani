About Our project

1.Less dependence on physical educational institutions as
 i) It takes a toll on natural resources.
ii) Economical constraints may hamper the development of physical institutions.
iii) For some, it can be challenging to physically go and attend physical institutions.

2.Inclusion of people from diverse economic strata.
 i) We strive to include people from diverse economic backgrounds by offering quality education (through alliance) to the masses.
ii) We strive to involve NGOs to give education and seek donations.

3.Bridging the gap between the job market and labour market.
 i) Our aim is to bridge the gap between jobs and the labour market for economic growth of a country.
ii) It is a promising factor for employees by giving the freshers the opportunity to upskill by working on hard skills and soft skills as required.
iii) This offers sustainability and growth on personal and professional level at the individualistic level that will ultimately make a big impact at a larger level.

- Reducing toll on natural resources
- Addressing economic constraints
- Overcoming challenges of physical attendance
- Providing quality education to all economic backgrounds
- Partnering with NGOs for education and donations
- Facilitating economic growth
- Upskilling freshers with hard and soft skills
- Promoting personal and professional growth

No Poverty

Our project clearly aligns with ‘No Poverty’ SDG by imparting employability skills via various portals. These skills are offered by NPTEL, SWYAM which are government-aided certifications. They are low on pocket, high on returns by imparting valuable skills for employability.

Quality Education

Our project strives to give quality education to the students. We facilitate courses via our platform offered by government and private educators, which are high in demand and help candidates to upskill and learn relevant skills and educate themselves by top-rated institutions.

Decent Work and economic Growth

This project opens up the door for recruiters to hire the skilled employees or to upskill and educate their current employees for better work collaboration and economic growth.

Reduce Inequality

Our project aims to offer quality education to all irrespective of their economic background, region, or gender. It strives to dissolve all the hindrances or biases to generate learning opportunities for all..

Sustainable Cities and communities

Our main aim is sustainable development where we aspire to provide quality education to all without exhausting our natural resources by limiting the need of the development of physical institutions.

Partnership for goals

We invite various government educational bodies, private educators, and institutions and NGOs for our endeavor. We believe that our collaboration would defy the obsolete padegogical approaches and work towards a better, sustainable growth model.
